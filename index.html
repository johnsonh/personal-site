About Me

I’m Johnson. I’m a writer, a feeler, a friend, a wanderer. My life has been shaped by questions: What makes someone feel safe? What gets in the way of intimacy? What do I need to feel at home in my body, in my relationships, in the world? Much of my journey has centered around my relationship with my mother, and my relationship to love, sexuality, masculinity, and truth. I write about all of it.
